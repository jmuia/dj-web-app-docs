1. Introduction

PartyAll is a web based application that allows guests at a party to democratically curate a playlist for the party they are attending. The purpose of PartyAll is to solve the issues and inefficiencies behind manually creating a playlist that a plurality of people agree on and to avoid having one person monopolizing the music. The issues and inefficiencies that manually creating a playlist for the party includes, but is not limited to, the fact that not everyone will be there at the start of the party to decide upon the songs, a group of people suggesting songs out loud at the same time will cause confusion and for some choices to go unnoticed by the group at large, and preselecting a song playlist means that the playlist will remain static throughout the party and not change as the tastes of the crowd change.

2. Object Classification

2.1 Entity Objects

  • Party - A playlist of songs that is curated by the Host and Guests.
  • Song - A piece of music that could be played at a Party.
  • User - A user of the PartyAll web application.
  • Host - The User hosting the Party.
  • Guest - A party-goer that wants to decide on the upcoming songs.

2.2 Boundary Objects

  • ChooseUserTypeView - Where a User selects their type, either Host or Guest.
  • HostPageView - Where a Host decides to create a new Party or log in to an existing Party.
  • HostCreatyPartyView - Where a Host fills out and submits a form requried to create a Party.
  • HostLoginView - Where a Host logs into their account.
  • GuestLoginView - Where a Guest logs into a Party.
  • HostPartyView - Where a Host views a Party.
  • GuestPartyView - Where a Guest views a Party.
  • SearchSongView - Where a User searches for a song.
  • SearchSongResultsView - Where a User sees the results of a search for a song.
  • SongView - Where a User views information relevant to a Song.

2.3 Control Objects

  • PartyController - Coordinates all activites related to Party objects.
  • UserController - Coordinates all activities related to User objects, and subsequently Host and Guest objects.
  • SongController - Coordinates all activities related to Song objects.
  • SearchController - Coordinates all activities related to searching for songs.
